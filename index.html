About the Studio

N.E. Atlanta's Original In-Home Music School

Zack Boland Music Lessons has been connecting students across Northeast Atlanta with passionate, skilled instructors since 2008. Our goal is to create the music school experience we wished we'd had — lessons that are fun, engaging, and uniquely tailored to each student.

Whether you're a parent looking to spark a lifelong love of music in your child, or an adult picking up an instrument for the first time, our team meets you exactly where you are.

Jessica Sisson

Saxophone, Clarinet & Music Theory Instructor
